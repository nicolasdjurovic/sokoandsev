About

Marko & Antonio (Soko & Sev) are a duo that seems as though they have come out of nowhere. Since releasing their first track on SayWhat? in May 2020 and within 12 months the pair has secured releases on some of Technoâ€™s most prolific labels including Codex, Kraftek, AnalyticTrail, IAMT and SetAbout just to name a few. Amongst hundreds of emerging artists the Serbian/Romanian duo have managed to establish their first year in the Techno scene as ones to watch in 2021, with their constant delivery of emotional, hard and almost anthem-like tracks to the scene.

Recently moving from London to Belgrade the pair have established bases in both countries and supporting top tier artists on stage such as Boris Brejcha, ANNA, Matador, Oliver Huntemann, Alex Stein, Juliet Fox, BEC & Anja Schneider the duo looks forward to 2021 as the year to showcase their hard work in the studio.
